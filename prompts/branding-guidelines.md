# Branding Guidelines

## Visual Consistency
All assets, including icons, photography, and UI components, must adhere to the **"Builder at the Edge"** persona. The visual vibe should be **"Dark, High-Tech, and Precise."** Use the core color palette (Deep Midnight Blue and Electric Cyan) consistently to create a unified technical aesthetic.

## Logo Usage
- **Primary Logo:** Should be a minimalist, geometric representation of a circuit or a bridge (symbolizing the Atoms and Bits duality).
- **Placement:** Top-left of the navbar.
- **Backgrounds:** Logo should always be displayed on a dark background to maintain contrast.

## Photography Style
- **Tone:** High-tech, cold-tinted (slightly blueish), professional yet candid.
- **Subjects:** 
    - Fariz in the field (e.g., at the airport, near BHS systems).
    - Fariz in a desk environment (surrounded by ESP32 boards, soldering irons, or multiple monitors).
- **Quality:** High contrast, sharp focus, with subtle bokeh but maintaining industrial detail.

## Tone of Voice
- **Technical yet approachable.**
- **Authoritative** (speaking from a position of expertise).
- **Problem-focused** (always highlighting the "Why" and "How" of engineering solutions).
