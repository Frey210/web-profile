# Professional Summary

## Elevator Pitch

Fariz Achmad Faizal is an Electrical Engineer specializing in Control Systems, IoT, and Industrial Automation, with hands-on professional experience across two high-stakes domains: airport critical infrastructure development and technology startup product leadership. He currently supervises the electronic systems commissioning of Sultan Hasanuddin International Airport's expansion while simultaneously leading R&D as CTO of PT Media Teknologi Celebes — building IoT platforms, industrial monitoring systems, and backend services from prototype to production.

## Key Expertise

1. **Industrial Electronic Systems & Quality Assurance** — Supervising PLC/SCADA, fire alarm, baggage handling, public address, X-ray screening, and data network systems in certified airport environments.
2. **IoT Platform Architecture & Embedded Development** — Designing end-to-end IoT systems from sensor firmware (ESP32, Arduino, STM32) to cloud backends and mobile dashboards.
3. **Backend Engineering & API Development** — Building production-grade REST APIs using Node.js, Django, and FastAPI with Docker-based deployments on Linux servers.
4. **Startup Product Leadership (CTO/Co-Founder)** — Leading a technical team through full product lifecycle: PRD, system design, embedded development, backend services, and deployment.
5. **Control Systems & Industrial Automation** — Applied experience in PLC programming (Allen-Bradley, Siemens), SCADA configuration, and field instrumentation within aviation and industrial contexts.

## Professional Mission

To engineer technology products that solve real, consequential problems — whether that means ensuring a shrimp farmer can monitor pond water quality from their phone, or ensuring that a baggage handling system in a major airport meets international safety and quality standards. Fariz aims to bridge the gap between deep embedded/industrial engineering and modern software development, building systems that are robust, maintainable, and genuinely impactful.

In the longer term, his mission is to grow PT Media Teknologi Celebes into a recognized technology company in Eastern Indonesia, creating high-quality engineering jobs and delivering world-class IoT and automation solutions to underserved industrial sectors.
