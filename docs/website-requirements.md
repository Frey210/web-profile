# Website Requirements

## Core Objectives

1. **Primary Goal — Attract high-quality career opportunities.** The portfolio website is the definitive professional calling card for Fariz Achmad Faizal. It should generate inbound inquiries from: recruiters at industrial/IoT companies, potential clients for PT Media Teknologi Celebes, collaborators for research projects, and conference/speaking invitations.
2. **Secondary Goal — Establish authority in the IoT & Industrial Engineering domain.** The site should communicate deep technical expertise — not just a list of skills, but demonstrated projects, published research, and real-world outcomes.
3. **Tertiary Goal — Showcase the startup (Aerasea/PT Media Teknologi Celebes)** as a credible technology company, not just a side project.

## Target Audience

**Primary:**
- Technical recruiters and hiring managers at engineering firms (aviation, IoT, automation, cloud)
- Decision-makers at companies seeking IoT or industrial automation consulting/development

**Secondary:**
- Fellow engineers and researchers in the IoT, embedded systems, and control systems space
- Investors or ecosystem builders interested in Aerasea/startup
- Academic collaborators and conference organizers

**Tertiary:**
- Undergraduate students looking for inspiration or mentorship in engineering

## Key Features

- **Interactive Project Showcase** — Full project case studies with architecture diagrams, tech stack badges, outcomes, and GitHub links; filterable by domain (IoT, Aviation, Robotics, etc.)
- **Downloadable CV** — One-click download of the latest PDF version of the English-language CV
- **Publications & Research Section** — Linked, formatted academic publications with abstracts and external links (DOI, Semantic Scholar, SINTA)
- **Achievements & Awards Timeline** — A visually engaging timeline or grid of awards, grants, and certifications
- **Dual-Role Narrative** — Clear communication of the "Airport QA Engineer + Startup CTO" dual career, which is the core USP
- **Responsive & Mobile-First Design** — Full usability on all screen sizes; many visitors will be on mobile
- **Dark Mode Interface** — Consistent with personal brand (dark, high-tech aesthetic)
- **Contact / Collaboration Form** — Clear call-to-action with email contact and professional social links (LinkedIn, GitHub)
- **Company / Aerasea Reference** — A brief section or link to PT Media Teknologi Celebes / aerasea.id
- **Certifications Section** — Displayed Dicoding certificates with verification links
- **Animated / Interactive Elements** — Subtle terminal-style animations, tech-aesthetic hover effects — consistent with the brand's "Builder at the Edge" persona

## Technical Constraints

**Must Use:**
- Next.js (React framework) — for SSR/SSG, SEO, and performance
- Tailwind CSS — for rapid, consistent styling
- Vercel — for deployment (zero-config, free tier, excellent DX)
- GitHub — for version control and CI/CD integration

**Must Avoid:**
- WordPress / Wix / Squarespace — too generic, insufficient control
- Overly complex CMS unless a blog is a priority (can use MDX or Contentlayer instead)

**Performance Requirements:**
- Lighthouse score ≥ 90 on Performance, Accessibility, and SEO
- First Contentful Paint (FCP) < 1.5 seconds
- All images optimized (WebP, lazy loading)

## Success Metrics

- **Monthly unique visitors:** Target 200+ per month within 6 months of launch
- **CV downloads:** 10+ per month
- **Inbound inquiries:** 1–2 quality professional inquiries per month via contact form or LinkedIn (referencing the portfolio)
- **Google Search Console:** Indexed within 2 weeks of launch; ranking for "Fariz Achmad Faizal" on page 1
- **Project page engagement:** Average session duration > 2 minutes (indicator of genuine interest in project case studies)
