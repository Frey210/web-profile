# Career Goals

## Short-term Goals (1–2 Years: 2025–2027)

- **Scale PT Media Teknologi Celebes** — Grow the startup to a team of 8–12 engineers, onboard 3+ enterprise clients in the aquaculture and industrial monitoring sectors, and achieve product-market fit for Aquanotes and Ergoquipt platforms.
- **Complete Airport Project Delivery** — See the Sultan Hasanuddin International Airport expansion through to final system commissioning, handover, and acceptance testing—building a strong reference case in aviation infrastructure QA.
- **Publish More Research** — Submit at least one additional peer-reviewed paper focusing on IoT sensor systems, computer vision applications, or aviation technology, strengthening the SINTA academic profile.
- **Deepen Industrial Automation Expertise** — Pursue formal training or certification in PLC programming (Rockwell/Siemens) and SCADA platforms, supplementing hands-on airport experience with verified credentials.
- **Strengthen Cloud Architecture Skills** — Move from basic AWS Cloud Practitioner knowledge to Solutions Architect level; implement more sophisticated cloud-native deployments for startup products.
- **Grow the Aerasea Brand** — Establish a strong digital presence for the company (aerasea.id) and position it as the leading IoT and automation technology company in Eastern Indonesia.

## Long-term Goals (3–7 Years: 2027–2033)

- **Postgraduate Research** — Pursue a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Control Systems, or IoT/Embedded Systems — either in Indonesia or internationally — to deepen theoretical foundations and expand the research portfolio.
- **International Projects** — Take on IoT or industrial automation projects with international clients or in international contexts, building a globally-recognized professional profile.
- **Product at Scale** — Develop Aquanotes or a derivative product into a commercially successful SaaS platform serving thousands of aquaculture farms across Southeast Asia.
- **Technical Co-founder / CTO at Larger Company** — Either grow PT Media Teknologi Celebes into a mid-sized technology company or transition into a CTO/Principal Engineer role at a high-growth Indonesian or global technology company.
- **Aviation Technology Innovation** — Develop and potentially patent a novel technology product or system in the aviation safety or air navigation domain, based on insights gained from AirNav internship and Angkasa Pura I project experience.
- **Research & Academic Impact** — Achieve meaningful academic citation counts and contribute to the development of engineering education at Universitas Hasanuddin or similar institutions as a guest lecturer or collaborating researcher.
