## Published Papers

### 1. SLAM-Based Mobile Robot Navigation — Y-BOT

- **Title:** *"Rancang Bangun Sistem Pemetaan dan Lokalisasi Berbasis Algoritma SLAM Menggunakan Depth Sensor Kinect pada Mobile Robot"*
  *(Design and Construction of a Mapping and Localization System Based on SLAM Algorithm Using Kinect Depth Sensor on a Mobile Robot)*
- **Journal:** JITET — Jurnal Informatika dan Teknik Elektro Terapan, Universitas Lampung
- **URL:** https://journal.eng.unila.ac.id/index.php/jitet/article/view/8070
- **Author Profile (JITET):** https://journal.eng.unila.ac.id/index.php/jitet/search/authors/view?givenName=Fariz%20Achmad%20Faizal&familyName=&affiliation=Universitas%20Hasanuddin&country=ID&authorName=Fariz%20Achmad%20Faizal
- **Semantic Scholar:** https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/RANCANG-BANGUN-SISTEM-PEMETAAN-DAN-LOKALISASI-SLAM-Faizal-Anshar/904b852ad81e199b420caf7bad712895ddb8af33
- **Google Scholar:** https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=RANCANG+BANGUN+SISTEM+PEMETAAN+DAN+LOKALISASI+BERBASIS+ALGORITMA+SLAM+MENGGUNAKAN+DEPTH+SENSOR+KINECT+PADA+MOBILE+ROBOT
- **Co-authors:** Anshar (supervisor/collaborator)
- **Abstract/Summary:**
  Presents the complete design and implementation of a mobile robot capable of autonomous mapping and localization using the SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) algorithm. The robot (Y-BOT) utilized a Microsoft Kinect depth sensor as the primary ranging sensor, integrated with ROS (Robot Operating System) for real-time environment mapping, self-localization, and autonomous navigation. The research validated the SLAM approach for the indoor navigation use case at Universitas Hasanuddin.
- **Year:** 2023 (undergraduate thesis research)

## Research Projects

### 1. Smart Broiler Chicken Coop — PKM-PI 2021

- **Project Name:** Smart Broiler Chicken Coop: Sistem Optimalisasi Masa Brooding Kandang DOC Ayam Broiler
- **Institution:** Universitas Hasanuddin
- **Funding:** Ministry of Education, Culture, Research and Technology of Indonesia — PKM (Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa) Scheme PI (Penerapan Iptek)
- **Grant Reference:** Certificate No. 6163/E2/KM.05.01/2021
- **Period:** 2021
- **Role:** Project Lead / Principal Researcher
- **Description:**
  Designed and built an IoT-based smart management system for broiler chicken coops targeting the DOC (Day-Old Chick) brooding period in Balong Village, Bulukumba Regency, South Sulawesi. The system optimized temperature, humidity, and lighting conditions using embedded controllers and automated actuators, with the goal of improving brooding survival rates and growth metrics for local farmers. The project applied technology to a real agricultural problem in a rural community setting.
- **Outcome:** National funding awarded; system prototype developed and deployed in community setting.

### 2. Smart Healing Aerochair — PKM-KC 2022

- **Project Name:** Smart Healing Aerochair: Inovasi Kursi Ergonomis Pengendali Stres dan Kelelahan Kerja Petugas ATC Menggunakan Citra Digital Berbasis Artificial Intelligence
- **Institution:** Universitas Hasanuddin
- **Funding:** Ministry of Education, Culture, Research and Technology of Indonesia — PKM Scheme KC (Karsa Cipta / Technology Creation)
- **Period:** 2022
- **Role:** Co-Lead Researcher / Technology Developer
- **Description:**
  Developed an AI-enhanced ergonomic chair designed specifically for Air Traffic Control (ATC) officers — one of the most stress-intensive professions in aviation. The chair used digital imaging and AI-based analysis to monitor physiological stress indicators (facial expression, posture, heart rate proxies) and automatically provided ergonomic adjustment and therapeutic responses to reduce fatigue and stress during long operational shifts. This research directly addressed workplace health in a safety-critical aviation context.
- **Outcome:** National funding awarded; AI-based prototype developed. The project inspired the later Ergoquipt product line at PT Media Teknologi Celebes.

### 3. Undergraduate Thesis — SLAM Mobile Robot (Y-BOT)

- **Title:** Rancang Bangun Sistem Pemetaan dan Lokalisasi Berbasis Algoritma SLAM Menggunakan Depth Sensor Kinect pada Mobile Robot
- **Institution:** Universitas Hasanuddin, Faculty of Engineering — Electrical Engineering
- **Repository:** https://repository.unhas.ac.id/id/eprint/47577/2/D041191091_skripsi_29-02-2024%20bab1-2(FILEminimizer).pdf
- **Period:** 2022–2024
- **Role:** Author / Lead Researcher
- **Description:**
  Completed a full undergraduate research project designing and building a SLAM-capable mobile robot (Y-BOT) using a Kinect depth sensor and ROS II. The robot autonomously mapped indoor environments and localized itself using real-time sensor data. The project covered mechanical design, embedded programming (Raspberry Pi + Arduino), ROS node implementation, and experimental validation of SLAM accuracy.
