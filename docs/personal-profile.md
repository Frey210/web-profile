# Personal Profile

## Biography

Fariz Achmad Faizal is an Electrical Engineer from Makassar, Indonesia, with a deep specialization in Control Systems, IoT, and Industrial Automation. He was born and raised in Makassar and completed his Bachelor of Electrical Engineering at Universitas Hasanuddin (2019–2023), graduating with a GPA of 3.79 / 4.00 — placing him among the top graduates in his cohort with a specialization in Control Systems and Instrumentation.

From his earliest years as a student, Fariz demonstrated a strong orientation toward applied technology: competing in national robotics contests, winning research funding from the Indonesian Student Creativity Program (PKM), and publishing academic research in international journals. He co-founded **PT Media Teknologi Celebes (Aerasea)** — a technology startup focused on aquaculture IoT, industrial automation, and software platforms — shortly after graduation, taking on the role of **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)**.

Today, Fariz operates in a dual professional capacity: he leads technology and product development at his startup while simultaneously serving as an **Electronic Officer (Quality Assurance)** at the **Sultan Hasanuddin International Airport development project** under PT Angkasa Pura I. In this role he oversees the commissioning and quality assurance of mission-critical airport electronic systems — including PLC/SCADA, fire alarm, baggage handling, public address, and data network infrastructure.

His engineering philosophy bridges the physical and digital worlds: he is equally comfortable writing firmware for an ESP32 microcontroller, designing cloud-native backend APIs, configuring enterprise networking equipment, and drafting Product Requirement Documents for product teams.
