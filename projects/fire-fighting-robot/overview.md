# Fire Fighting Robot — Overview

## Problem Statement

The KRSRI 2022 national robotics competition requires an autonomous robot to navigate a prescribed arena, detect fire sources (candle flames), and extinguish them. The challenge demands integration of legged locomotion, autonomous navigation, real-time flame localization, and actuated suppression in a single embedded system.

## Solution

A STM32-based hexapod (6-legged) autonomous fire-fighting robot with tripod gait locomotion, UV/IR flame detection, and fan/pump suppression, capable of completing a KRSRI-specification competition course.
