# Fire Fighting Robot — Results & Links

## Results

- Functional hexapod fire-fighting robot entered in **KRSRI 2022** national university robotics competition
- Demonstrated STM32 bare-metal C programming, 12-servo PWM control, gait algorithms, and sensor-based autonomous behavior
- Highest complexity embedded system built during undergraduate period in terms of mechanical + firmware integration
