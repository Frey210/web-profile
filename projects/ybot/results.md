# Y-BOT — Results

## Academic Outcomes

- ✅ **Undergraduate thesis completed and approved** — Universitas Hasanuddin, 2024
- ✅ **Peer-reviewed journal paper published** — JITET, Universitas Lampung
- ✅ **Indexed in** Semantic Scholar, Google Scholar, SINTA

## Technical Outcomes

- Successfully demonstrated real-time SLAM mapping in an indoor environment using Kinect depth camera
- Validated the depthimage-to-laserscan conversion pipeline for SLAM on low-cost hardware
- Demonstrated autonomous waypoint navigation within the generated map
- Proved viability of Kinect (low-cost) as a replacement for LiDAR in SLAM applications for educational/research robotic platforms
