# Y-BOT — Contribution

## Role
**Lead Researcher · Robot Designer · ROS Developer · Author**

This was Fariz's **undergraduate thesis project** — designed, built, and programmed independently under academic supervision.

## Contributions

- **Full mechanical design** — designed and assembled the mobile robot platform from scratch
- **Electronics integration** — Raspberry Pi + Arduino + Kinect + motor driver wiring and interfacing
- **ROS II software development** — wrote and configured all ROS nodes: Kinect driver, laser scan converter, SLAM integration, navigation stack, motor controller bridge
- **SLAM implementation and validation** — tuned SLAM parameters, evaluated mapping accuracy in real indoor environments at Universitas Hasanuddin
- **Academic writing** — authored the undergraduate thesis (Skripsi) and journal paper published in JITET

## Academic Output

- **Thesis (Skripsi):** Submitted and approved by Universitas Hasanuddin, Faculty of Engineering (2024)
- **Journal Paper:** Published in JITET — Jurnal Informatika dan Teknik Elektro Terapan (Universitas Lampung)
