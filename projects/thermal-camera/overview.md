# Thermal Camera — Overview

## Problem Statement

Air conditioning systems waste energy by running at full power even when rooms are empty. A smarter system should detect human presence and adjust AC setpoints automatically for energy efficiency and comfort.

## Solution

Thermal camera-based human presence detection using infrared imaging and OpenCV computer vision. Detects body heat signatures in a room and automatically adjusts AC temperature setpoints accordingly.

## Key Features

- Thermal IR-based human presence detection (no privacy issues — captures heat maps only)
- Automated AC control based on occupancy and thermal comfort estimation
- Energy-efficient room climate management
- OpenCV-based blob detection for thermal hotspot analysis
