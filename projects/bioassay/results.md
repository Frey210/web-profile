# Bioassay Color Mapping — Results

## Outcomes

- Demonstrated that plankton density can be reliably classified using RGB color characteristics of water samples
- Provides a low-cost alternative to laboratory microscopy for aquaculture plankton monitoring
- Validated KNN classifier accuracy against field reference samples
- Complementary technology to the Aquanotes IoT platform — adds biological monitoring dimension to physicochemical water quality monitoring
